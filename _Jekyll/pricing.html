Features you are getting with every plan

Unlimited domains, mailboxes, and logins!

When we say unlimited, we mean unlimited! Doesn't matter how many domains you have, or how many mailboxes, or email addresses, Oikik can handle all of them. Just add domains and recipient addresses, point your mails to our MX and relax :)

State-of-the-art spam blocking

Using our advanced spam filtering technology, customers will see more than 99% of all spam messages will never reach your inbox. Our antispam system also features a Neural Network based model, so it'll gradually learn newer spam mail patterns and keep your inbox safe 24/7.

Personalized Filtering

You have full control over the spam filter. If you don't want filtering for a trusted email address, we got you covered. Using our web app, you can manage all of your domains, recipient addresses, Whitelists etc, and also check mail logs. We'll also send a daily report of the emails that were rejected as spam.
