Get Started, It's easy!

Just follow these 3 simple steps and you're good to go!

Add Your Domain


Add a new domain and verify that you are the domain admin

Add Recipient Address


You can choose for which specific addresses we should check for spam. If you want filtering for all addresses of that domain, just write @yourdomain.com on the Recipient Address field

Redirect your mails to our MX


Change your domain's MX DNS records to: @ IN MX 10 mx1.oikik.io. and @ IN MX 20 mx2.oikik.io. so we can start filtering your mail!
